You are loved

Louisville Youth Group has been working for over 25 years to promote and facilitate a universal community that accepts, affirms and uplifts all young people regardless of their sexual orientation and gender identity.

>5000

In more than 25 years of service, LYG has served over five thousand lgbtq and allied youth in the Greater Louisville area.

>200

LYG provides mentoring, support services, health education, and more, to more than 200 lgbtq and allied youth each year.
